ABOUT

Hannah Cannizzo is a graphic designer and multimedia artist based in NYC. She attendex Parsons School of Design and is based in New York. She is adept in book and online media design, and has created a slew of books and websites tailored to specific themes and aesthetics. Moody photos, muted colors, and collages are seen peppered throughout her work. She also shoots digital and film photography, focusing on portraiture. Hannah is intrigued by the idea of humanism and simplicity, portraying unspoken dialogue between people in a multitude of mediums. A storyteller at heart and designer by trade, Hannah combines her multifaceted practices to showcase everyday, and often at first glance plain, human experiences.
